Aircraft TOW prediction

Open source model for European air traffic management

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an open-source model for predicting aircraft Take-Off Weight (TOW) in European air traffic management systems. The goal is to improve flight planning efficiency and safety through accurate weight predictions.

Technical Approach:

We developed a machine learning model that analyzes various flight parameters to predict aircraft take-off weight. The model considers factors such as route distance, weather conditions, aircraft type, and historical data to provide accurate predictions.

Visualization of the aircraft TOW prediction system and its components

Key Features

  • Real-time TOW prediction for multiple aircraft types
  • Integration with existing air traffic management systems
  • Weather and route-based weight adjustments
  • Historical data analysis and pattern recognition

Results

Our open-source model has demonstrated significant improvements in prediction accuracy compared to traditional methods. The system is now being used by several European air traffic management organizations to enhance their flight planning processes.
